## Who We Are

TenRec is an AI research lab dedicated to developing safe, beneficial artificial intelligence. Founded in 2026, we bring together researchers from machine learning, computer science, and related fields to tackle some of the most important problems in AI.

## Our Approach

We believe that advancing AI capabilities and ensuring AI safety are complementary goals, not competing ones. Our research spans fundamental machine learning, reasoning and planning, and alignment — with each area informing the others.

We publish our research openly and contribute to the broader scientific community, because the challenges of building beneficial AI are too important and too large for any single organization.
